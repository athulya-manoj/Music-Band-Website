Crescendox Symphonica Rhythm

Pop Music

Music lessons for everyone's choices who want to learn music in a Rhythm way

Crescendox is a South Korean boy band formed in 2025. The band consists of Albin, Felix, Atlas, Tate, and Mike, who co-write or co-produce much of their material. Originally a hip-hop group, they expanded their musical style to incorporate a wide range of genres. Their lyrics focus on subjects such as mental health, the troubles of school-age youth, coming of age, loss, the journey toward self-love, individualism, and the consequences of fame. Their discography also references literature, philosophy, and psychology, with an alternate universe storyline.
